Privacy Policy

How Air Shooter handles data.

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Information We Collect

Air Shooter does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal information from users.

The app does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or payment information.

Local App Data

Air Shooter may save small amounts of data locally on your device to support gameplay features, such as:

  • Sound and music settings
  • Pause or resume state
  • Saved score or game progress

This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers. Removing the app may delete this local data.

Audio

Air Shooter uses audio features to play sound effects and background music. The app does not record audio or upload audio from your device.

Third-Party Services

Air Shooter does not include advertising, analytics, user tracking, or third-party login services in the current version.

Children's Privacy

Air Shooter is intended as a general audience game. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

Because Air Shooter does not collect personal information, we do not store personal information on external servers. Local game data is stored on your device by the app.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app changes. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact
