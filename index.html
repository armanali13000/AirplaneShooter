About

Designed for clean, intense air combat.

Air Shooter keeps the screen readable and the action direct, so each run is easy to start and satisfying to improve.

Fly through hostile skies, avoid incoming enemies, collect powerful upgrades, and keep pushing your score higher.

The game includes pause and resume support, sound controls, music, power-ups, enemy movement, and custom game assets.

Developed by Arman.

Gameplay

Everything is built around quick decisions.

Move, shoot, dodge, collect, and survive. The loop is simple, but every run gets more demanding as the screen fills with danger.
